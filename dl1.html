Dialogue about a picnic they have enjoyed

X :Hello, I have heard that you get up early in the morning and walk for quite a long time.

Y :Yes I always get up minimum 1 hour before the sunrise and go for a morning walk.

X :Is there any benefit of early rising?

Y :Of course! An early riser has many adventages. He can start his daily worlk earlier. As an early riser has enough time to work, he can earn more and become wealthy.

X :Is early rising good for health?

Y :Yes! The man who rises early in the morning can take some exercise and walk in the morning fresh air in the open field.The morning air refreshes our body and mind.

X :I was completely in the dark. From now, I will start rising early in the morning.

Y :If you cultivate the habit of rising early. You will realise it's important.

X :Okay. Thank you very much

Y :You are most welcome.
