Dialogue about a picnic they have enjoyed

X :Hello, Y! How are you? How did you enjoy the picnic

Y :Yes I am fine and the picnic was really exciting and amazing. It was more enjoyable to me because I never went to Garo Pahar before.

X :I had also never been to Garo Pahar before. Our journey was very enjoyable. The place was literally wonderful.

Y :I had curosity to know the life style of the Garo People. My long cherished dream has been fullfilled. Their life style simply amazed me.

X :Yes. The lunch was also a great attraction to all of us. We were very hungry. So, we took our lunch at our heart's content. The song, dance and their culture also added to our pleasure.

Y :You have taken a lot of snaps. haven't you?

X :Yes, here are the photographs.

Y :The picnic was so enjoyable that it ill peep in my mind for the rest of my life.

X :Same to me. Thank you very much. Bye

Y :Bye
